😴 Sleep Better

  • 🌙 Same bedtime every night
  • 📱 No screens 30 min before bed
  • 📖 Read a book instead
  • 🧸 Keep room dark and cool
  • 🎵 Try soft music or nature sounds

😰 Handle Stress

  • 🫁 Take 5 deep breaths
  • 🗣️ Talk to someone you trust
  • 📝 Write down what's bothering you
  • 🏃 Get some exercise
  • 🎨 Do something creative

👫 Make Friends

  • 😊 Smile and say hello
  • 👂 Be a good listener
  • 🤝 Share and take turns
  • 💪 Stand up for others
  • 🎮 Invite someone to play

📚 Study Smart

  • ⏰ Study in 25-min sessions
  • 📋 Make a to-do list
  • 🍎 Take healthy snack breaks
  • ✋ Ask for help when stuck
  • 🎯 Set small, achievable goals

💪 Build Confidence

  • 🗣️ Use positive self-talk
  • 🏆 Celebrate small wins
  • 🆕 Try new things
  • 🪞 Say something nice about yourself
  • 📝 Keep a success journal

🍎 Stay Healthy

  • 💧 Drink lots of water
  • 🥗 Eat fruits and veggies
  • 🏃 Exercise daily
  • 😴 Sleep 8-10 hours
  • ☀️ Spend time outdoors
